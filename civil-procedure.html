👩‍🏫 Meet Your Professor

Professor Eleanor Whitmore
Professor of Civil Procedure

Former federal appellate clerk and civil litigator, Professor Whitmore has taught at Aldercrest University since 2014. She is known for her rigorous Socratic classroom discussions, practical approach to civil litigation, and dedication to mentoring first-year law students.

📢 Welcome to Civil Procedure

Welcome to your first semester at Aldercrest University College of Law. Civil Procedure introduces the rules that govern lawsuits in the United States, including jurisdiction, pleadings, discovery, motions, and trials.

Read Chapter 1 of the course text and review the syllabus before our first class meeting.
