Course Description

This course introduces the rules governing civil litigation in the United States. Students will study jurisdiction, pleadings, discovery, motions, trial procedure, judgments, and appellate review while developing the analytical skills expected of first-year law students.

Learning Outcomes

  • Understand the structure of civil litigation.
  • Interpret the Federal Rules of Civil Procedure.
  • Analyze procedural issues in legal disputes.
  • Develop legal reasoning and advocacy skills.
  • Prepare for upper-level litigation courses.
